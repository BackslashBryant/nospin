NoSpin Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the NoSpin browser extension.

Data collected

None. NoSpin does not collect personal data. It does not send your browsing history, article text, or page content to any external server.

What is stored

Only user settings are stored, on your own device, in chrome.storage.local (for example: global on/off, per-site overrides, and other preference values needed for the extension to work).

That data is never transmitted by NoSpin and is not synced to the cloud.

Analytics, tracking, and third parties

NoSpin does not use analytics, tracking pixels, advertising networks, or third-party telemetry. There are no external services invoked for analysis or data collection.

Accounts

No accounts or sign-in are required.
