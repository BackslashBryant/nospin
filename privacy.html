NoSpin Privacy Policy

This policy applies to the NoSpin browser extension.

What data NoSpin collects

NoSpin does not collect personal data. It does not send your browsing history, article text, or page content to any external server.

What is stored locally

NoSpin stores only extension settings on your device in chrome.storage.local, such as:

  • global enabled or disabled state
  • per-site overrides
  • other local preference values required for extension behavior

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted by NoSpin.

Analytics and third-party services

NoSpin does not use analytics, tracking, advertising pixels, or third-party telemetry services.

Accounts and sign-in

NoSpin does not require user accounts and does not provide sign-in.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through GitHub issues.
