NoSpin

See when wording may be pushing a story.

NoSpin is a browser extension that surfaces structural spin signals in news writing—patterns in how a story is framed—so you can stay grounded and judge the reporting for yourself.

What NoSpin detects

One-sided sourcing
Vague authority
Unsupported causality
Identity-salience framing
Denominator-free statistics
Headline-body tension

What NoSpin does not do

  • It does not collect your data or run analytics.
  • It does not track you across sites.
  • It does not send article text or page content to any server.
  • It does not require an account or sign-in.
  • It is not a fact-checker and does not decide what is true.
  • It does not call external services for analysis.

How settings are stored

User preferences live only in chrome.storage.local on your device. Nothing is transmitted for storage or sync.
