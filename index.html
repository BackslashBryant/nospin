NoSpin

See when wording may be pushing a story.

NoSpin highlights structural signals in news writing so you can stay grounded and read with your own judgment.

Available on the Chrome Web Store Privacy policy

What NoSpin detects

One-sided sourcing or source imbalance
Vague authority claims without support
Unsupported causality framing
Identity-salience framing language
Headline-body tension
Denominator-free statistics

What NoSpin does not do

  • It is not a fact-checker and does not decide what is true.
  • It does not collect or transmit your article content.
  • It does not require an account, sign-in, or tracking profile.
Built for readers who want clearer language signals, not louder takes.
